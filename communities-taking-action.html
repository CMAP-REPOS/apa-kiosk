Local Technical Assistance program

CMAP launched a Local Technical Assistance (LTA) program in March 2011, with support from a three-year, $4.25 million competitive grant from the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) as part of the federal interagency Partnership for Sustainable Communities.

Through the LTA program, CMAP staff are helping communities to implement the GO TO 2040 comprehensive regional plan. This overview video features several of the initial projects, including:

  • Comprehensive plan for the Village of Campton Hills
  • Land use planning and redevelopment of the old Joliet Prison site
  • Green and Healthy Neighborhood Plan for the City of Chicago's neighborhoods of Englewood, Washington Park, and Woodlawn

Nearly 100 projects have been initiated since the program's inception.
