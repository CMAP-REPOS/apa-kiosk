This interactive map briefly describes each project recommended for congestion pricing in GO TO 2040. It also includes anticipated results for morning peak travel periods, based on CMAP computer models.

  • Illinois Route 53 north extension and Illinois Route 120 bypass

    CMAP MODELING RESULTS

    Trip Modeled: IL 53/120 extension from Schaumburg to Waukegan at morning peak (23 miles)

    Target Speed: 45 mph

    Average Express Toll: $0.29 per mile (compared to anticipated general-purpose toll of $0.20 per mile)

    Time Savings: 31 minutes faster than current travel times

    Arterial Benefit: 10-percent less congestion
