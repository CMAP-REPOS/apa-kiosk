LaGrange Park Jr. High student projects

The 167 students in Ms. Sydney Schuler's 8th grade STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) class at LaGrange Park Jr. High School spent two weeks learning about the GO TO 2040 comprehensive regional plan's recommendations to Promote Sustainable Local Food, culminating in an open house to share their implementation ideas with local mayors and CMAP officals, as well as family and friends.
